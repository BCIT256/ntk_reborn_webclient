# Splash Video

Place your splash screen video here as `splash.mp4`.

- **Path:** `public/assets/splash/splash.mp4`
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec recommended for broad browser support)
- **Duration:** ~8 seconds
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 or higher (will be scaled to fill screen)

The client will autoplay this video (muted) on first launch.
When the video ends, it will smoothly fade into the title screen.
