# Title Screen Music

Place your looping title screen background music here as `title_music.mp3`.

- **Path:** `public/assets/title/title_music.mp3`
- **Format:** MP3 or WAV
- **Duration:** Any (will loop seamlessly)

The client will play this music on loop while the title screen is visible.
When the user presses any key to enter, the music will fade out over 1.5 seconds.
