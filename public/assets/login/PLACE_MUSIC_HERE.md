# Login Screen Music

Place your login screen background music here as `login_music.mp3`.

- **Path:** `public/assets/login/login_music.mp3`
- **Format:** MP3 or WAV
- **Duration:** Any (will loop seamlessly)

The client will play this music on loop while the login screen is visible.
When the user logs in, the music fades out over ~1.5 seconds.
